Dear Saiyam,

You are one year older now and a little bit more socially acceptable. It is crazy how fast time passes and it’s crazy how it alters things and how precious somebody could be to someone. Just know that you have me at the brink of a call and I’ll be there for you. I cannot wait to see you and smother you with kisses.

Thank you for being who you are,
Palak <3
